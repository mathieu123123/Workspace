What is Camp ‘C’ the
Future?
Your child is invited to participate in our annual Centennial Regional High School Summer Readiness Camp. The goal of the camp is to prepare Grade 6 students for high school. Participants will have the opportunity to spend one week taking part in exciting activities led by a group of extremely capable and enthusiastic Secondary 4 and 5 students, and a team of energetic staff members. Throughout the week of camp, we will familiarize our future Centennial students with the ins and outs of the Secondary 1 experience. They will take part in the traditional sports and games that you would find at any great camp. They will have the opportunity to take advantage of the amazing Creative and Performing Arts facilities at Centennial Regional High School. They will have the chance to discuss and learn how they can be
 
comfortable, involved and successful in their first year of high school.
